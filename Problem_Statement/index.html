The Data Story

Geographic, demographic, and behavioral patterns reveal the root causes of customer attrition

Geographic Risk Profile

Germany emerges as the highest-risk market requiring immediate attention

Key Insight
German customers churn at 32.4% vs 16% in other markets. Potential causes: stronger competition, different customer expectations, or service gaps requiring market research.

Age-Based Vulnerability

Middle-aged customers (40-60) show highest attrition risk

Strategic Implication
Peak earning customers (40-60) are most vulnerable. This demographic typically has highest lifetime value, making their retention critical for revenue protection.

Product Portfolio Impact

Sweet spot at 2 products; dangerous extremes at 1 or 4+ products

Optimization Opportunity
Customers with 2 products have lowest churn (7.6%). Those with 1 product (27.7% churn) need cross-selling; those with 4+ (100% churn) may be over-serviced.
